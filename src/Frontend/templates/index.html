Process

Three steps to clarity

No sign-ups. No complexity. Just honest answers and a clear picture of where you stand.

01

Answer 10 questions

Each question explores a different dimension of your mental well-being — stress, sleep, mood, and more. Simple, honest options. No right or wrong answers.

02

We analyse your responses

Your answers are processed by our system. Each response is weighed against established well-being indicators to build an accurate picture.

03

See your result

You receive a clear mental health snapshot with context — what it means for you and what steps you can consider going forward.

Answer honestly

This tool works best when you reflect genuinely. Your responses are private and used only to generate your personal result. There is nothing to gain from choosing any particular answer.

  • Take your time with each question
  • Go with your first instinct
  • Think about the past two weeks
Academic project notice: MindCheck is built as a DBMS academic project. It is not a clinical tool and does not replace professional mental health evaluation. If you are experiencing distress, please consult a qualified mental health professional.
